FAQ/Help

Got questions? This might help.

Q: May I still call to make an appointment?

A: Of course! Call our receptionist at (850)-644-7278 and they will be happy to make an appointment for you using the scheduling tool.

Q: What time are Friday walk-in hours?

A: Advisors are available for walk-in hours on Fridays from 8:00am-5:00pm. No appointment needed!

Q: If I make an appointment using the scheduling tool can I cancel it?

A: The tool allows for cancellations up to one day prior to the appointment. If a student tries to cancel an appointment the day before it is supposed to happen, they will not be allowed to do so.

Q: What if I have an emergency the day before my appointment?

A: If the circumstances call for an emergency cancellation, call the receptionist at (850)-644-7278 and cancellation will be granted.

Q: May I choose which advisor I want to see using the scheduling tool?

A: When you sign up for an appointment using the tool, there is an option to leave a comment. Leave your preferred advisor in the comments and you can meet with them if they are available during this time slot.
